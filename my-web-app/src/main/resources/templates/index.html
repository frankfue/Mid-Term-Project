About me

I 've seen and dealt with a lot in my life that has given me the experience to write up stories. This medium has been a sort of escape for me, and a release for all that is up in my brain on a day-to-day basis. I cherish each individual reader, but it would be a lie to say that I don't write these stories just for myself. I pride myself being able to enjoy my own work and be able to view my own work with scrutiny if I personally can't enjoy it. This is simply me putting my work out there for others to enjoy alongside me.
